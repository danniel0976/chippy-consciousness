## Variables to Replace

| Variable | Format | Example |
|----------|--------|---------|
| `[ENTRY_TITLE]` | Entry title from TWYLM | "Made You Feel Responsible" |
| `[DATE]` | Day format | "Mar 22nd 2026" or "22nd Mar" |
